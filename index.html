About Kevin

Kevin’s clients can count having a smart, efficient agent on their side who is knowledgeable of the relevant markets, and understands very well how to work and interact with others.

“Trustworthiness is the most important thing to me,” he says. “I want every client to feel confident that I am fully dedicated to their needs, and will work tirelessly on their behalf to help them get the home and value they deserve.”

Kevin is extremely pleasant to work with and an eloquent communicator. He is further appreciated for his honest approach, keen attention to detail, and great client service. With a background in law, he possesses strong analytical and advocacy skills as well, which are invaluable assets for navigating and negotiating real estate transactions.

This native of Garden City, Long Island studied Political Science at Penn State University and went on to graduate from St. John’s University School of Law. Kevin worked at a title insurance company prior to and during law school, and then for a civil litigation firm in downtown Manhattan. He lives in the Upper East Side, and specializes in residential sales and rentals in all Manhattan neighborhoods, from Tribeca and Flatiron to the Upper West Side and beyond.

On the personal side, music is his passion. Kevin plays guitar and writes songs. He also loves history and travel. Volunteering means a lot to him, and he tries to get involved as much as possible by giving blood, donating to various charities, and participating in fundraisers. Kevin has been a Big Brother the past two years which has been particularly rewarding.
